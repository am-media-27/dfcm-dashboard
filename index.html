Teams notifications

How to get the URL (takes 2 minutes):

1. Open Microsoft Teams
2. Go to the channel where you want notifications
3. Click the (...) next to the channel name
4. Select "Workflows"
5. Search for "Post to a channel when a webhook request is received"
6. Click it and select your Team + Channel
7. Click Save
8. Copy the webhook URL it gives you
9. Paste it here

Note: Old-style connector URLs (outlook.office.com/webhook/...) also work until April 2026.
